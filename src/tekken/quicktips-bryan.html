(3,3,4)

A common cheesy string Bryans use is (3,3,4), which is a mid-high-mid string.

While this is -12 on block, it is only punishable at the wall due to its huge pushback. Bryans often bait a whiff with the pushback and punish accordingly.

To counter this, either sidestep the last 4 in either direction, or duck the high and interrupt the string.
